2020

Rare is the third solo studio album by Selena Gomez who self-described the album as her "diary from the past few years".

Rare is a midtempo pop and dance-pop record, taking cues from electronic, Latin pop and R&B. Its lyrics generally address themes of self-love, self-empowerment, self-acceptance, and self-worth.

Hotel Transylvania 3 is an animated monster comedy film. In the film, Dracula finds love with a ship captain named Ericka while on a vacation on a cruise liner with his family and friends, but Mavis later discovers that Ericka is actually the great-granddaughter of Abraham Van Helsing, Drac's arch-enemy.

Selena Gomez reprises her role as Mavis Dracula: Drac's daughter, Johnny's wife, and Dennis' vampire mother

2015

Revival is the second solo studio album Selena Gomez. The record was influenced by a range of artists, particularly Christina Aguilera and her album Stripped (2002).

The album reflects her journey since 2013, including the media scrutiny of her personal life. Lyrically, the album discusses themes of love and confidence.

2013

Stars Dance is the first solo album by Selena Gomez. The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 and sold 97,000 copies in its first week of release in the country.

Selena promoted the album through a number of high-profile performances, such as the 2013 Billboard Music Awards. She embarked on the Stars Dance Tour (2013) to further promote the release.

Wizards of Waverly Place is an American fantasy teen sitcom that aired on Disney Channel

The series centers on Alex Russo (portrayed by Selena Gomez), a teenage wizard living in Greenwich Village, Manhattan, who undertakes training alongside her siblings, Justin and Max, who are also equipped with magical abilities. The siblings are trained knowing that one day they will compete to win sole custody of their family's powers.
